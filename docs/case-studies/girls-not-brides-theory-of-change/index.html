Girls not Brides interactive Theory of Change

Girls Not Brides is a global partnership of more than 400 civil society organisations from over 60 countries working to address child marriage. The Theory of Change is informed by the knowledge and experience of more than 150 Girls Not Brides members and experts about what it will take to end child marriage.

In collaboration with digital strategist, Adam Brown, we devised and developed an interactive version of the Theory of Change that makes it a valuable tool in the organisation’s media toolkit.
