Topshop quiz

28 February 2015
admataz case studies - Topshop quiz

After the success of the quiz admataz developed for the Kate Moss campaign in 2014, the digital team at Topshop were keen to repeat the success with a similar quiz in their 2015 online magazine. Topshop commissioned a new quiz, dubbed “What’s your Tribe” to provide a fun way for users to browse the themed trends for the season.
